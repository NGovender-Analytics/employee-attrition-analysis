# Employee Attrition Analysis
## HR Analytics | Business Impact Report

### Overview
Analysis of employee attrition patterns across a 1,470-person
organisation identifying key turnover drivers and quantifying
financial impact.

### Key Findings
- Turnover Rate: 16.1%
- Employees Who Left: 237 out of 1,470
- Estimated Annual Cost: R155,352,427
- Employees who left earned $2,046 less per month on average
- Research and Development had highest attrition (130 employees)
